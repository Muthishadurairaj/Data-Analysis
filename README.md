# Data-Analysis

Store Analysis is an interactive Excel dashboard that analyzes sales performance using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

# Project Overview

The Store Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, order status, and regional trends. By leveraging Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel formulas, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights, enabling informed decision-making.

# Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to evaluate the store's sales performance by identifying revenue trends, customer purchasing patterns, channel performance, and order fulfillment efficiency. The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve sales, optimize inventory, and enhance customer satisfaction.

# Dashboard Components

1. Sales Ratio
* Compares Monthly Sales Amount with the Number of Orders.
* Tracks sales trends across the selected months.
* Helps identify peak and low-performing periods.
  
2. Online Platform Purchases
* Analyzes the number of purchases made through various online platforms.
* Compares the performance of sales channels such as Ajio, Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Nalli, and Others.

3. Top 5 States
* Displays the five highest revenue-generating states.
* Provides insights into regional sales performance.

4. Order Status
Shows the distribution of orders based on their current status:
* Delivered
* Returned
* Refunded
* Cancelled
Evaluates the overall order fulfillment performance.

5. Men vs Women Analysis
* Compares sales generated by male and female customers.
* Further categorizes customers into Adult, Senior, and Teenager age groups.
* Interactive Filters

# Key Insights

* Adult customers contribute the largest share of total sales.
* Women generate higher sales revenue compared to men.
* Myntra, Flipkart, and Amazon are among the leading online sales channels.
* Maharashtra is the highest revenue-contributing state among the top five states.
* The majority of orders are successfully delivered, indicating efficient order fulfillment.
* Monthly sales remain relatively consistent, with moderate fluctuations across the selected period.

# Business Recommendations
* Focus marketing campaigns on adult customers, particularly women, who contribute the highest sales.
* Strengthen partnerships with high-performing online sales channels to maximize revenue.
* Maintain adequate inventory levels for high-demand product categories.
* Increase promotional activities in top-performing states to sustain revenue growth.
* Continue improving delivery performance to enhance customer satisfaction and reduce cancellations or returns.
* Utilize interactive filters for periodic performance reviews and strategic planning.
  
# Conclusion

The Store Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of the store's sales performance. By combining sales trends, customer demographics, online channel performance, regional analysis, and order status into a single dashboard, it enables efficient business monitoring and supports data-driven strategic decision-making. The dashboard demonstrates the effective use of Microsoft Excel's analytical and visualization capabilities to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.
